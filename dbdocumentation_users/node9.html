Data

After data is submitted and reviewed by a database admistrator, it is made available for analysis. These data are suitable for basic scientific research and modeling. All previously published, reviewed data are made publicly available to users of BETY-db who are conducting primary research. Access to these raw data is provided to users based on affiliation and contribution of data. We hope to promote the field in two ways; first, by allowing other users access to the data that we have collected, and second, by providing a streamlined interface that allows users to enter their own data in a standardized format with automatic meta-data that stores links data and associated information.
