Yields

Yields (tab:yields) includes aboveground biomass in units of Mg ha-1. Biomass harvested in the fall and winter generally represents what a farmer would harvest, whereas spring and summer harvests are generally from small samples used to monitor the progress of a crop over the course of the growing season. Managements associated with Yields can be used to determine the age of a crop, the fertilization history, harvest history, and other useful information.
